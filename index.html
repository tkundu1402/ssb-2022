Here's a timeline of Pichai Sundararajan life:

  • Pichai Sundararajan was born June 10, 1972 Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India
  • He complete studies at IIT Kharagpur (BTech) Stanford University (MS) University of Pennsylvania (MBA)
  • He worked in engineering and product management at Applied Materials and in management consulting at McKinsey & Company
  • He had been suggested as a contender for Microsoft's CEO in 2014, a position that was eventually given to Satya Nadella
  • In August 2017, Pichai drew publicity for firing a Google employee who wrote a ten-page manifesto criticizing the company's diversity policies
  • In December 2019, Pichai became the CEO of Alphabet Inc
  • In 2022, Pichai received the Padma Bhushan from the Government of India, its third-highest civilian award
  • Pichai is married to Anjali Pichai and has two children
